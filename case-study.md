# 🏠 BAVIST Real Estate Management Platform
## Case Study

---

## 📌 Overview
BAVIST is a real estate management platform designed to help property owners and managers efficiently handle property listings, tenant information, rent tracking, and maintenance requests in one centralized system.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Real estate management is often fragmented across spreadsheets, messaging apps, and manual tracking systems. This leads to inefficiency, missed payments, poor communication, and difficulty managing multiple properties at scale.

---

## 💡 Solution
BAVIST provides a centralized digital platform that simplifies real estate operations by enabling users to:
- Manage property listings
- Track tenant information
- Monitor rent payments
- Handle maintenance requests
